Manduu

The pulse of fitness.

What They Needed

As the first FDA-approved electrical muscle stimulation training program in the U.S., Manduu is revolutionizing the way people pursue health and wellness. With its personalized fitness regimen, small classes and state-of-the-art studios, Manduu is a safe and most effective alternative to a traditional gym.

What We Delivered

We helped Manduu build its brand from the ground up, building the website, creating compelling national advertising campaigns and client testimonial videos. We also work with individual Manduu franchises around the country to cement brand awareness, boost appointments and engage clients through effective email campaigns, social media management and multimedia advertising.

The Result

Our first year managing Manduu’s social media led to huge increases on Facebook and Instagram, including 307% more followers and 233% more engagement combined.
